This is what my spaghetti looks like. It's perfectly cooked and the meatballs are homemade. :)

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Noodles
  • Sauce

Instructions:

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add noodles to water
  3. When noodles are finished.. strain noodles
  4. Open jar of sauce and pour into pot of noodles
  5. Enjoy :)
